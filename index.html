About me

About me

Artur Boronat is a lecturer at the Department of Informatics of the University of Leicester (UK). He holds a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV,Spain). He has been a visiting researcher at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC, USA) and at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC, Spain).

His current h-factor is 13 (according to Scopus).
